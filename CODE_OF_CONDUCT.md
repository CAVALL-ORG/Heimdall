# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct. The full text is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: we are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity. Participants are expected to act
professionally and respectfully in all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions) and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainers at
**ethan@cavall.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly, and the reporter's privacy will be respected. Maintainers are obligated
to keep the details of any incident confidential.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may
face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the project's
leadership.

For the complete set of community standards, enforcement responsibilities, and
escalation guidelines, see the linked Contributor Covenant 2.1 text above.
